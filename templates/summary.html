Simple Interest

Formula

SI = (P × R × T) / 100

Amount = P + SI

Where:

  • P = Principal amount
  • R = Rate of interest per annum
  • T = Time period in years
  • SI = Simple Interest

Example

If P = ₹1000, R = 10%, T = 2 years

SI = (1000 × 10 × 2) / 100 = ₹200

Amount = 1000 + 200 = ₹1200

{% else %}

Compound Interest

Formula

A = P(1 + R/100)^T

CI = A - P

Where:

  • P = Principal amount
  • R = Rate of interest per annum
  • T = Time period in years
  • A = Final Amount
  • CI = Compound Interest

Example

If P = ₹1000, R = 10%, T = 2 years

A = 1000(1 + 10/100)² = 1000 × 1.21 = ₹1210

CI = 1210 - 1000 = ₹210
